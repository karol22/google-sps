Hi, I'm Karol!

I am a senior Computer Science student from Mexico. My main interests are programming and mathematics. I love to build things from scratch and learn new things. I want to develop something that helps many people as well as bring technology to the youth of Mexico.

Background

I lived in different small towns and cities when I was a child and I had no idea what to do with my life. Then, in 2010 I discovered math contests and started a romance with science and technology. In 2015, I wrote my first code in C++ and after that I realized how amazing it would be to be a programmer. I learned a little more, started with programming contests and in 2016 I got a full scholarship to study Computer Science in a great college here in Mexico.

I'm in my last year of college and currently my favorite subject is Data Science because I love all the math that is involved. I'm also collaborating with a professor in a research project of bioinformatics, this is a new kind of challenge to me and if it ends well I'll share it with you.

Besides college, I work part in a fintech startup of online loans. This is quite cool to me since it is like a mini bank and I can work everyday with people from different areas such as engineering, data science, marketing, etc. Thanks to this job I have learned many web skills such as Ruby, Golang, Javascript and some frameworks.
